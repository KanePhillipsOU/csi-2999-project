More Parks

Stage Nature Center

Friendly staff and miles of trails, Stage Nature Center has activities for the whole family. The park is full of deer, and houses a number of rescued owls.

Tenhave Woods

Trails are covered with wildflower in spring.

Kensington Metropark

Has a fishing, trails, a nature center, cranes, a farm, and a golf course.

Johnson Nature Center

Has a lake, miles of trails, and historical buildings on site.
