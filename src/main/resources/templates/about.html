About

Binary Boulevard
Pigeon Hole Praire, UI 16180


Bug Free Campground is located off of Binary Boulevard in Pigeon Hole Praire, UI 16180. Bug Free Campground was established on January 1st, 1970. The first camping season took place in 1972, where the campground only offered 5 sites! Due to the campgrounds popularity, expansion began in 1975 installing hookups in the first 2 loops of the park. Linux Lake was opened for swimming in 1990, the trails were installed in 2007 with more additions in 2015. All campsites were rented on a first-come first-serve basis until 2024 with the addition of a new website for campground reservations.

Now, Bug Free Campground offers an etensive trails system with over 15 miles of hiking and biking trails, a beach for swimming and lakes and rivers for fishing. The campground has 99 sites with full-hookup, water and electric, and rustic ammenties available. It is our goal for you to have the best experience possible, whether you travel alone or with your family. So kick your feet up, and enjoy your home-away-from-home at Bug Free Campground!

Come explore the natural world with Bug Free Campground!

Rules and Regulations

Reservations and Check-in

  • Check-in time is 2:00 PM. Check-out time is 12:00 PM.
  • All campers must register at the campground office upon arrival.
  • Campsites are assigned upon registration. Changing sites without approval is prohibited.

Quiet Hours

  • Quiet hours are from 11:00 PM to 6:00 AM.
  • Loud music, generators, and other noise-producing equipment must be turned off during quiet hours.

Visiting Hours

  • Visiting hours are from 8:00AM to 8:00PM.
  • Visitors must purchase a day pass from the contact station.

Vehicles and Parking

  • The speed limit within the campground is 15 MPH.
  • Park only in designated areas. Do not park on the grass or in non-designated spots.
  • Limit of two vehicles per campsite.

Natural Resources

  • Do not damage trees, plants, or other natural features.
  • Stick to marked trails and avoid creating new paths.
  • We are compliant with DNR restrictions on fishing. See their website for updated procedures and more information.

Misc. Info

  • The campground is mowed on Tuesdays. Please be sure to remove lawn decorations and furntiture to avoid damage.
  • Mail cannot be sent to the campground.
  • Information about local shops and attractions will be provided upon request at the contact station.
