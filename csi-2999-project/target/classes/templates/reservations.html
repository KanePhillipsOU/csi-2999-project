Bug Free Campground is located off of Binary Boulevard and Syntax Street in Pigeon Hole Prairie, UI. Bug Free Campground offers an extensive trail system with over 15 miles of hiking, biking, and equestrian trails. We offer swimming, fishing, and kayak and row boat rentals available from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM Friday-Monday. Come explore the natural world with Bug Free Campground!
