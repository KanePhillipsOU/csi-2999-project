About

Bug Free Campground is located off of Binary Boulevard and Syntax Street in Pigeon Hole Praire, UI. Bug Free Campground offers an etensive trails system with over 15 miles of hiking, biking, and equestrian trails. We offer swimming, fishing, and kayak and row boat rentals available from 10:00AM to 5:00PM Friday-Monday. Come explore the natural world with Bug Free Campground!
