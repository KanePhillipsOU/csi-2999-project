More Parks

Stage Nature Center

With friendly staff and miles of trails, Stage Nature Center has activities for the whole family. The park is full of deer, and houses a number of rescued owls.

Tenhave Woods

Tenhave Woods has 22 acres of protected land trails, a plethoral of wildflowers in the spring, mushrooms in the fall, and qr codes to learn more about nature.

Kensington Metropark

Come enjoy Kent Lake with beaches, fishing, boating, trails, and abundant wildlife. Also check out events at the nature center, golf, and disc golf.

Johnson Nature Center

Check out Johnson Nature Center's forty-acres with views of lakes, miles of trails, and historical buildings on site.
